Happily Ever After first year

Posted on 22 Oct 2016 by dontfollowmeimcrazy

Hello guys!

Today I’m writing just two lines to “celebrate” the first year of Luca Poldelmengo’s full-length video, Happily Ever After. Right on this day, a year ago, HEA was premiered at Cinema Visionario in Udine. It was a great honour for me to partecipate on a such great project.

All the material we produced is online and available in this Tumblr blog. Take a twenty minute break from your life and have a look at the video.

See ya!
